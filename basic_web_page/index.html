Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes, Guild Event Walkthrough: Hoth - Imperial Invasion

Overview

The first Guild Event in the popular mobile game Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes (SW:GoH), it provides an opportunity for a guild to provide a host of rewards for the entire guild in a collective effort. Guild Events require communication and application of each guild member's resources in an effort to complete various goals including assignment to platoon missions, combat missions, special missions and general deployment. Rosters refresh after each phase (6 phases) and units are employed in increasingly more difficult ways and with more strict requirements.

The 6 phases breakdown down into 15 sections in total, where the guild can earn up to three stars per section up to a maximum of 45 stars. Only the guilds with the highest participation, communication and well-developed characters and ships will be able to achieve all 45 stars in the time of the event. Breakdown

Platoon/Squadron Missions

For platoon and squadron missions, each guild member can assign up to ten (10) troops and ships, respectively, to a platoon of 15 characters or ships selected by the server. For each platoon and squadron completely filled, the guild collects the assigned amount of territory points (TP) and progresses toward the goal of that mission. For every two (2) platoons completed, the guild gains some ability or boost to achieve other objectives.

Combat Missions

Combat missions pit a squad of your troops or ships against an increasing more difficult waves of enemies with various special abilities and chances to be an Elite unit with even more abilities to defeat your squad. Each mission attributes the units assigned to attempt it as deployed power for that area as well as additional territory points (TP) for each wave cleared.

Special Missions

Special missions are considerably more difficult, but, if completed, they provide the guild with valuable rewards such as guild event tokens (guild event currency) or shards to progress in unlocking and improving various new characters.

General Deployment

Once all of the other missions have been completed or the guild member's resources are assigned, it is up to the guild member to assign the rest of their units accordingly.
